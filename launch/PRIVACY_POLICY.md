# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026**

## Plain-English summary

Purdue RMP — Inline Professor Ratings does not collect, store, or transmit any
personal information about you. It does not track you, does not use analytics,
and does not touch your student records, login, grades, or registration data.

The only data the extension sends anywhere is a **professor's name and school**,
which it sends to RateMyProfessors' public service to look up that professor's
ratings. Everything else — cached ratings and your settings — stays on your own
computer, inside your browser.

That's the whole story. The details below just spell it out.

---

## What the extension does

Purdue RMP runs only on Purdue's UniTime scheduling site
(`timetable.mypurdue.purdue.edu`). It reads instructor names that are already
displayed on that page and shows their RateMyProfessors ratings inline.

## Information we collect

**None about you.** The extension does not collect, request, or store:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Purdue login credentials or account details
- Courses you view, register for, or are enrolled in
- Grades, transcripts, or any student records
- Browsing history
- Analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking of any kind

## Data the extension reads and sends

- **Instructor names on the page.** The extension reads professor names that are
  already shown on the public UniTime scheduling page in order to know which
  ratings to look up.
- **Professor name + school sent to RateMyProfessors.** To fetch ratings, the
  extension sends only a professor's name and the school (Purdue) to
  RateMyProfessors' public GraphQL endpoint
  (`https://www.ratemyprofessors.com/graphql`). No information about you is
  included in these requests.

When you contact RateMyProfessors through this extension's lookups, RateMyProfessors'
own privacy practices apply to data they receive and to standard request
metadata (such as your IP address) inherent to any web request. This extension
adds no personal data of its own to those requests.

## Data stored locally

The extension stores the following locally in your browser using
`chrome.storage.local`. This data never leaves your device:

- **Cached ratings.** Professor ratings the extension has already looked up, so
  it doesn't have to re-fetch them. Cached entries expire after a short period.
- **Your settings.** Preferences such as on/off toggles and display options.

You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension or clearing its
storage from Chrome's extension settings.

## What we do NOT do

- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising
- No selling or sharing of data
- No remote servers operated by us (the extension has no backend of its own)

## Third-party service

The extension fetches ratings from **RateMyProfessors**
(`ratemyprofessors.com`). Their data and privacy practices are their own. This
extension is not affiliated with or endorsed by RateMyProfessors.

## Children's privacy

The extension is intended for use by university students and does not knowingly
collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published alongside the
extension's listing and source.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the extension developer through the
